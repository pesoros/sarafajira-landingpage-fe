Sara Fajira

The songstress was born in City Of Works (Surabaya) at May 3, 1996. She grew up in family that embraces art and music. Sara’s brother, which became instrumental in many creative process behind her music, was a musician in Surabaya indie music scene. While her sister, Naykilla, had just started her own way as singer.
